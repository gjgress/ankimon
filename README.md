# Ankimon

Ankimon is an Anki addon that gamifies your learning experience by allowing you to catch, collect, train, and fight with Pokémon within the Anki environment. With Ankimon, learning becomes an adventure where you can enhance your knowledge while having fun.

## Features

- **Pokémon Collection:** Catch and collect Pokémon as you progress through your Anki decks.
- **Training:** Train your Pokémon to improve their abilities and strengths.
- **Battles:** Engage in battles with other users on Pokémon Showdown to test your knowledge and skills.
- **Interactive Learning:** Ankimon integrates seamlessly with Anki, enhancing your learning process by adding an element of excitement and challenge.
- **Customizable:** A wide range of settings allows you to customize your Ankimon experience, from the appearance of your trainer to the difficulty of the battles.
- **Cross-platform:** Ankimon is compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux.

## Installation

1. **Download and install Ankimon:** You can download the latest version of Ankimon from the [releases page](https://github.com/h0tp-ftw/ankimon/releases).
2. **Download the necessary resources:** You will need to download the data files, sprite files, and badges and item sprites. You can do this by going to `Ankimon > Help > Download Resources` in the Anki main window.
3. **Restart Anki:** Once you have installed the addon and downloaded the resources, restart Anki to complete the installation.

## Usage

Once you have installed Ankimon, you can start catching Pokémon by reviewing your Anki cards. As you review your cards, you will encounter wild Pokémon that you can catch and add to your collection. You can then train your Pokémon and battle them against other users on Pokémon Showdown.

The Ankimon menu in the Anki main window provides access to all of the addon's features, including:

- **Game:** This menu contains options for starting a new game, managing your backups, and accessing the item shop.
- **Profile:** This menu contains options for viewing your trainer card, your achievements, and your Pokémon team.
- **Collection:** This menu contains options for viewing your Pokémon collection, your Pokédex, and your item bag.
- **Export:** This menu contains options for exporting your Pokémon to Pokémon Showdown and PokePaste.
- **Help:** This menu contains options for accessing the Ankimon user guide, reporting a bug, and viewing the addon's credits and license.

## Contributing

Ankimon is a community-driven project, and we welcome contributions from everyone. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a complete beginner, there are many ways you can help to make Ankimon better.

### I don't know how to code! Where do I start?

You don't need any prior coding experience to contribute to Ankimon. As long as you have a passion for Ankimon and are willing to learn, you can make a valuable contribution. Here's how to get started:

1. **Join our Discord server:** Our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/eY8jPHZw4z) is the best place to get help and connect with other contributors.
2. **Learn the basics of Python:** Ankimon is written in Python, so it's a good idea to learn the basics of the language. The [W3schools Python tutorial](https://www.w3schools.com/python/default.asp) is a great place to start.
3. **Explore the Ankimon codebase:** Once you have a basic understanding of Python, you can start exploring the Ankimon codebase. The `src/Ankimon/functions` directory is a good place to start, as it contains many of the addon's core functions.
4. **Start using AI:** AI can be a powerful tool for learning to code and for contributing to open-source projects. You can use AI to get help with coding, to learn how to guide AI, and to spot errors in your code.

### Instructions for New Contributors

1. **Fork this repository:** Click the "Fork" button at the top right of this page to create your own copy of the repository.
2. **Make your changes:** Make your improvements or fixes in your forked repository.
3. **Submit a Pull Request:** Push your changes to your fork and then open a pull request to merge your changes into the `main` branch of this repository.

After your first accepted pull request, you will be granted maintainer access to this repository, which will allow you to help shape the future of the project.
